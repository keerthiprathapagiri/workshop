INFO ABOUT FISHES

Fishes are aquatic animals that live in water and breathe through gills. They are found in oceans, rivers, lakes, and ponds. Most fishes have fins that help them swim and scales that protect their bodies.

Fishes are an important part of the food chain and provide food for humans and other animals. They come in many shapes, sizes, and colors, and some can survive in both fresh and salt water.

Swordfish

Swordfish are large, predatory fish known for their long, flat bills that look like a sword. They are fast swimmers and usually found in warm ocean waters.

Speciality: Their long sword-like bill helps them hunt and defend themselves, making them one of the most powerful predators in the ocean.

King Fish

Home

   

King Fish are popular food fish known for their rich taste. They are commonly found in coastal waters and are widely used in cooking.

Speciality: They are highly valued for their taste and are widely used in seafood dishes across many countries.

Anchovy

Home

   

Anchovies are small, oily fish that are often used in sauces and pizzas. They are usually found in schools in the ocean.

Speciality: They are rich in omega-3 fatty acids and are commonly used to enhance flavor in many dishes.

Salmon

Home

   

Salmon are well-known for their pink flesh and high nutritional value. They migrate from ocean to rivers to lay eggs.

Speciality: They are famous for their long migration journey and are one of the healthiest fish due to high protein and omega-3 content.

Catfish

Home

   

Catfish are named for their whisker-like barbels. They usually live in freshwater and are known for their adaptability.

Speciality: Their whiskers help them sense food in muddy water, allowing them to survive in low-visibility environments.
